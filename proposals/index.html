Engagement proposal

Digital presence
built for the next decade

ODH has spent years building a reputation as one of Venezuela's most respected voices in economic, political, and social analysis — with a client roster that speaks for itself. What's missing is a digital surface that matches the work. This proposal outlines a four-week engagement to build exactly that.

Context

Why this matters now

Venezuela's reopening has triggered a wave of inbound interest from foreign investors, multilateral institutions, and corporates evaluating market entry. They will all do the same thing first: search for trusted local analysts.

Right now, a Google search for ODH returns fragments. A search for "Venezuela political risk consultancy" returns competitors. The firm's expertise exists; its digital footprint doesn't. The goal of this engagement is to close that gap quickly, professionally, and without distracting ODH's team from client work.

Scope of work

The Essential package

A complete bilingual digital foundation: website, search visibility, and LinkedIn presence — designed and delivered together so they reinforce each other.

01

Bilingual website — 5 pages

A custom-designed site that positions ODH at the level of its actual client roster. Built for credibility first, lead capture second. English and Spanish, with clean toggle between languages.

  • Home, About, Services, Insights/Publications, Contact
  • Custom design aligned with ODH's existing logo and identity
  • Bilingual content (EN/ES) — written, not machine-translated
  • Mobile-responsive, fast-loading, secure hosting setup
  • Contact form connected to ODH's preferred inbox
02

SEO + AI engine optimization

Search infrastructure built in from day one so the site can actually be found — not just on Google, but on the AI tools that increasingly answer the first question a prospect asks. When someone types "best Venezuela political risk consultancy" into ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, or Gemini, ODH should be in the answer.

  • Keyword research targeting Venezuela advisory, political risk, and economic analysis queries (EN + ES)
  • On-page SEO across all 5 pages — titles, meta, schema, internal linking
  • Generative Engine Optimization (GEO): structured content, clear topical authority, FAQ schema, and citation-ready formatting that AI engines pull from
  • Google Search Console + Analytics setup
  • Google Business Profile creation and optimization
  • Technical baseline: sitemap, robots.txt, Core Web Vitals pass, llms.txt for AI crawlers
03

LinkedIn presence — company + leadership

The company page and key leadership profiles rebuilt as a coordinated system. This is where 80% of qualified prospects will look before they ever reach the site.

  • LinkedIn Company Page — full rebuild with banner, About, specialties, hashtags
  • Up to two founder/partner profile optimizations (headline, About, Featured, Experience)
  • Bilingual copy across all assets, aligned with website voice
  • Custom banner design matching ODH's brand
  • Five post templates ODH can adapt and publish independently
Timeline

Four weeks, not more

Week 01

Discovery & strategy

One working session with ODH leadership. Positioning, target audiences, content review of existing publications and case material. Sitemap and design direction approved by end of week.

Week 02

Design & bilingual copy

Site design across all 5 pages. Bilingual copywriting (EN/ES) drafted in parallel. SEO keyword strategy finalized. First design review at end of week.

Week 03

Build & LinkedIn rollout

Site development. LinkedIn company page and profile assets built and staged. SEO infrastructure (Search Console, schema, GBP) configured. One revision round.

Week 04

Launch & handover

Site goes live. LinkedIn assets published. Walkthrough session: how to update content, how to publish posts, how to read analytics. Documentation delivered.
